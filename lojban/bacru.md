## ロジバン-日本語辞書
x₁ は x₂ （声／音）を発する  
意思疎通や話し相手は必ずしも含意しない。

## ロジバン-英語辞書
x₁ utters verbally/says/phonates/speaks [vocally makes sound] x₂.  
Also voices; does not necessarily imply communication or audience; ('says' is usually {cusku}).
